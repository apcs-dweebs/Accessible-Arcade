Games for those who are colorblind or have anxiety!
This project was created for High Tech Hacks 2021.

Puzzle 1: Beginner level for red-green color blindness

    There are 4 puzzles arranged by difficulty and color blindness.
    Drag and drop the pieces from the left to the boxes in the right to make the final image.

About the puzzles:

    Many people have these 2 main blindnesses (red/green, blue/yellow)
    Barely any colorblind puzzles/games exist
    Feels more rewarding, accomplished than normal games for those with color blindness in the end → See a vibrant image
    Has been tested through research see below for link
    There are four puzzles: 2 difficulty levels for both red-green and blue-yellow color blindness
